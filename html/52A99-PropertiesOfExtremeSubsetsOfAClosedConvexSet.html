properties of extreme subsets of a closed convex set


Let K be a closed convex subset (http://planetmath.org/ConvexSet) of a normed vector space

  1. 1.

    If {Ai:i∈I} is a family of extreme subsets of K, such as ⋂i∈IAi≠∅ then ⋂i∈IAi is extreme subset of K

  2. 2.

    A⊂B⊂K such as A,B are extreme subsets of B and K respectively. Then A is an extreme subset of K.
